Mistakes to Avoid

Critical pitfalls that undermine AI effectiveness.

⚠

Over-Reliance

Avoid depending solely on AI. Human judgment remains essential for context, nuance, and accountability.

🚧

Ignoring Limits

Be aware of AI tool limitations. Not every problem has an AI solution — know when to stop and verify.

📚

Lack of Training

Ensure proper training on tools before use. Unskilled deployment leads to poor outputs and wasted time.

✍

Poor Prompting

Craft clear, specific prompts for better results. Vague inputs produce vague outputs — learn prompt engineering.
